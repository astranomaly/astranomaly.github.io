About

One day, when I was in Kindergarten, I drew an ant.

It was at that point that I discovered I really liked this "Art" thing. I grew up thinking I was going to be a cartoonist (or maybe an astronaut or a chemist). Turns out I wasn't that far off! These days instead of drawing pictures of ants, I combine a small bit of computer science, a heap of artistic creativity, and a can of Arizona black tea into my own form of artistic expression.

Even though I primarily focus on web design I occasionally find myself drawn into the realms of mobile apps and games. And while I'm not as adapted to those realms as I am to the Web, I plan to explore them further in the future (because the world might just end if I stopped exploring).

To give you an idea of what I'm familiar with...
- I'm very comfortable using Photoshop & Sublime
- I often use HTML5, CSS3, & jQuery
- Sometimes I use PHP, JSON, AJAX, & MySQL
- I've done a fair bit of work with Illustrator & Flash in the past
- It's been a while since I last used AS2/AS3
- I plan on speeding up development with SASS & Compass
- I'd like to learn Java, MySQLi, & Python

As far as non-Web related hobbies go, I'm a photographer, occasionally dabble in making music, and spend a lot of time daydreaming about road trips & hiking.
