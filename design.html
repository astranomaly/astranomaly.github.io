Graphic Design

Modifying the physical world

Information

Graphic design is what lead me down the Web Development path in the first place, and occasionally people ask me to do graphic designs for them. Business cards & logos mostly. Luckily for me, graphic & web design are closely related and both were part of my college education.

Over the past few years I've been contracted by companies of many sizes, with customer reaches that span from private to local community to province-wide. Most recently, I updated ViaBar's website and packaging, along with shooting their product photography. I also designed and printed mail flyers and business cards for Reimer Carpentry.

Technologies

The packaging designs for ViaBar were done exclusively in Adobe Illustrator, with Adobe Photoshop being used to touch up the product photography. The work for Reimer Carpentry was done using a mix of Illustrator and Photoshop. Recently, I've moved over to Affinity Designer & Affinity Photo for all my design work.
